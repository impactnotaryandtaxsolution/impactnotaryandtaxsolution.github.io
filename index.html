How is Notary Public different from a Loan Signing Agent?

A Notary Public witnesses signatures and charges a fee per signature notarized.

A Loan Signing Agent is a Notary Public who walks a borrower through loan documents for the sale/purchase of a home or a refinance. They don't charge per signature notarized, instead, they charge a flat fee for their skill & knowledge in executing a loan signing package.
